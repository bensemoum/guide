How to Watch IPL 2022 in Pikashow App

You can easily watch IPL 2022 in your Pikashow app by visiting the sports section of this app. in the sports section; you will see a live matches option click on it. After this, you will see all the live matches streams; select the stream you want to watch.

If you want to watch Live IPL in pikashow, click on the watch IPL and choose any free server to stream IPL 2022.
