How Can You Take Pets?

There are small pets featured in this Toca Life Word game, known as the crumpets. We will let you know how you can acquire them as our next tip from the Toca Life Word tips list. Pets are usually lying under the covers of the manhole, or they hide in the trees. They all have a range of appearances and species with them, and the area you find them are the places they belong to.
