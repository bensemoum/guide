Toca Life World Beginner’s Guide

Toca Life World starts with the steps to create a character. This will be the player’s first character, and the game will suggest multiple images to choose from. You can also build up some new heroes with the Life CityToca and know that this game has an inbuilt character system on it as well.

The next part of the game will be dedicated to taking players into their first apartment, and you will start the adventure here. The house will be where players are greeted by the main characters called Nari and Rita. You see eight heroes in the beginning, and players can create images for anyone from them.

So many locations are also placed in this Toca Life World that you can roam around, and players can try out their abilities for the interaction and movement with their heroes.

Toca Life World also has a post office on it, and once you have been gifted, you will see a red circle that features above your house. Presents can be taken by going into this post office and by tapping the transport container as it features the gifts on the tape.

If you have no idea how to create a hero, then that is no problem. You can still have a pre-made character, and by entering the buildings in your city, you can choose several heroes to choose. Selecting your heroes can be done with a broader range of selecting as follows.

  • Adults
  • Mythical creatures
  • Teenagers
  • Children
  • Pets
  • Aged people

    • You can interact and equip things to your home in this Toca Life World gameplay, and by utilizing the hammer option, you will be able to build new buildings. The game will offer you several gifts, and you can collect them from your mail. These gifts can be further used to reveal mini secrets and have new things purchased from the store.
