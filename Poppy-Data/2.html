What is Poppy Playtime?

Poppy Playtime is a first-person horror game in which the gamer assumes the role of a former Playtime Co. employee who returns to the abandoned toy factory after acquiring an odd message from the crew who vanished ten years earlier.

As it turns out, the factory’s toys are suddenly alive and actively seeking to cause harm. The designer included additional VHS videos for playback throughout the facility to offer a more thorough description of Poppy Playtime’s narrative.

Some challenges in the game will require the use of a gadget known as a GrabPack; a bag with two extended hands that may be used to grab things that are far away, pull yourself up, swing, and more. Originally, Chapter 1 cost $2.99. However, since the release of Chapter 2, the devs decided to give the first installment for free.
